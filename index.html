The So Yummy project includes React and Node.js technologies. This project consists of a Frontend and Backend.

So Yummy is a recipe database. Users can browse recipes, add them to their favorites, and add ingredients from a recipe to a shopping list.

The first step is registration. You need to register with your name, email, and password. After that, the next step is email confirmation. My app sends you an email with a link to confirm your registration. Once confirmed, you can log in.

After logging in, you have access to search for recipes by keywords and categories. On the main page, you can see the most popular recipes across several categories.

In this project, I developed the entire backend. I used Mongoose to communicate with the MongoDB database. I also created API endpoints to connect with the frontend.

On the frontend, I developed the sign-in, registration, and main pages. On the main page, we display the most popular recipes, divided into categories, along with an input field to search by keyword or ingredients.

The next step was creating recipe cards, favorites, and shopping lists, and setting up communication between components.

I used hooks like `useEffect`, `useParams`, and `useState`. For storing environment variables, I used "dotenv", and for the database, I used MongoDB.
